[holidays](https://github.com/vacanza/holidays) is the standard Python library for
generating government holiday calendars, downloaded 28M+ times a month.

**Saint Vincent and the Grenadines support (PR #2608, merged).** I added complete
holiday definitions for Saint Vincent and the Grenadines — researching the country's
statutory public holidays, encoding the rules (including movable observances), and
covering them with the project's test conventions so the calendar stays correct
year over year.
